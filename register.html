Matrix

The open, decentralized communication protocol. Full control, end-to-end encryption, and federation across servers.

Pros
  • Decentralized & federated — no single company controls it
  • End-to-end encryption available in private rooms
  • Interoperable with Discord, IRC via bridges
  • Fully open-source, community-driven, privacy-first
Cons
  • Can feel slow in large rooms or on low-end devices
  • Some metadata exposed to homeserver
  • Client UX varies (Cinny, Commet, etc.)
  • Joining the right rooms can be tricky for beginners

Fluxer

A Discord-like experience with strong privacy. Channels, roles, voice/video calls, screen sharing — all self-hosted.

Pros
  • Familiar Discord-like experience — easy transition
  • Privacy-first, open-source, no ads
  • Lightweight & fast — smoother than heavy clients
  • Good community tools: roles, permissions, audit logs
Cons
  • Newer product (~2026) — still maturing in polish
  • Centralized by default (self-hostable but single-instance)
  • No federation yet — rooms don't federate across instances
