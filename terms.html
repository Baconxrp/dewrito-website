We strive for a welcoming, respectful community across both Matrix and Fluxer features. Violations may result in content removal, channel bans, or account suspension. Expectations: Be friendly, patient, welcoming, considerate, respectful. Honor diversity (race, gender, orientation, disability, etc.). No discrimination. Resolve disagreements constructively.

Fluxer features are provided for personal, community, or group use (aligned with AGPLv3 open-source licensing). Self-hosting gives full control, but you must follow these rules:

  • Use for lawful, non-abusive communication: text chat, voice/video calls, screen sharing, custom emojis/stickers, roles, etc.
  • Commercial hosting/monetization (e.g., charging for access, premium features, or "Plutonium"-style tiers on your instance) is allowed only if you comply with AGPLv3 (provide source code) and our general prohibited activities (no high-risk businesses).

  • Prohibited: Spam/bots flooding channels; generating/sharing CSAM, explicit adult content (banned under general rules), hate speech, threats, or illegal material; circumventing moderation tools.
  • Excessive resource use (e.g., overloading VoIP servers, bulk spam calls) may result in throttling or suspension.
