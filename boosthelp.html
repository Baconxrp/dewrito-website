How to Boost Your Community

Get your Matrix room or Fluxer server featured on the Dewrito homepage slider and community directory using decentralized ledger rewards.

Our promotional showcase operates completely on chain. By sending a qualifying transaction over the Stellar network, our automated daemon tracks metrics and pushes your listing directly into our high-visibility rotation slider.

Step 1: Obtain Your Memo Key

Every community registered in our database is assigned a unique tracking token identifier key.

  • Navigate to the main Boosted Communities portal page.
  • Fill out the details to Create Your Boost Memo, and double check everything.
  • Copy the exact text string shown under the "Promo/Memo Key" label.
Important Configuration Note: You must provide this procise memo or it will not showup. If you have issues, contact support.
Step 2: Submit Ledger Boost Transaction

Each individual slot boost transaction tier adds 30 days of active visibility validation status.

  • Base Tier Rate: 25 XLM balances exactly 1 full promotion month cycle.
  • Issue the transaction from your preferred asset wallet to our infrastructure hub node address:

  • Ensure your wallet interface transaction has a Memo Field.
  • Paste your copied Memo into the input field line.
Step 3: Verification and Live Sync Loop

Once network blocks reach completion consensus (typically within 3 to 5 seconds):

  • Our verification pipeline records the balance updates instantly.
  • Your community expiration window calculation dynamically updates on the backend.
  • The top 3 highest tier boosted active communities automatically populate the main page ⚡ Active Boosts rotation array module slots.

Content Guidelines & Restrictions

To keep the platform safe and functional for everyone, listings matching flagged profanity filter arrays or possessing explicit content classifications are stripped during database processing blocks and will be restricted from displaying publicly inside the landing index sliders. Keep titles, names, and summaries fully clean to ensure continuous visibility cycle rotation.

There are no refunds, your donation goes to the Dewrito Team. We will try to help you get listed to the best of our ability, just so long as the content isn't in violation of our terms.
